# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 7, 2026

## Overview
This extension processes screen recordings locally on your device. It does not collect, transmit, or share personal information.

## Data Collection
We do **not** collect, store, or sell any personal data, usage data, or analytics.

## Data Handling
All recording and export processing happens **locally** in your browser. Files are saved directly to your device via the browser download dialog.

## Data Sharing
We do **not** transmit any data to external servers or third parties.

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not use third-party services or SDKs for analytics, tracking, or data processing.
It includes the open-source `gif.js` library bundled locally for GIF export.

## Permissions
Permissions are used only to enable screen capture and saving files locally:
- `tabCapture` to capture the current tab
- `downloads` to save files
- `offscreen` to process recordings
